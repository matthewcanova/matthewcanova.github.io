Audio Data Mining

This was a pair project for a Data Mining class. It was a fairly naive project and was motivated by my desire to learn about Speech Recognition (despite a direct course not being offered). The idea was to use Data Mining techniques (Machine Learning techniques were not allowed for this project) to attempt to meaningfully classify/cluster audio data. We targeted two features, words and speakers. We were unsuccessful in meaningfully clustering or classifying either, but we learned a lot about audio pre-processing for Speech Recognition along the way. :)
